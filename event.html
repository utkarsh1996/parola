The Verbal Crusade

Event type: Conventional Debate


The Verbal Crusade is a conventional debate event, wherein speakers will come in teams of two and debate on a motion that has been given to them in advance.


Topic: To be released a week before the event.


Speaking time: 3+1 minutes


Judgement criteria will be the same as that of any Conventional Debate.

LOK SABHA

Event type: Simulation of the Lok Sabha


An educational simulation in which participants implements their knowledge of Indian Politics and Diplomacy.


An event which includes researching, public speaking, debating and writing skill in addition to critical thinking, team work and leadership qualities


It is same as that of a conventional Indian MUN committee, with portfolios being assigned to each participant.The agenda is 'Strengthening the Indian foreign policy with regards to changing geopolitical situation.'

U.P. STAKEHOLDERS' MEET

Event type: Simulation of the Stakeholders' Meet in the context of Uttar Pradesh.


The U.P. Stakeholders' Meet is an committee set up to critically analyse and discuss the important issues pertaining to the state government. It has multiple facets and plays a key role in forming the policies of the government. A key aspect of this committee is that the citizens of the state participate in it and contribute in the policy making procedure.


It is an unconventional committee, with one portfolio being assigned to each participant. Its agenda is 'Reviewing the performance of the BJP Government.

WHITE HOUSE SITUATION ROOM

Event type: Simulation of the White House Situation Room.


An educational simulation in which participants implements their knowledge of American policy and politics.


An event where the room is equipped with secure, advanced communications equipment for the President to maintain command and control of U.S. forces around the world.


It is an unconventional committee, with one portfolio being assigned to each participant. Its agenda is ‘classified’.

CYNOSURE

Event type: A bilingual poetry slam.


A chance for each participant to present their aesthetic and rhythmic knowledge of literature, Cynosure brings you a bilingual slam poetry.


• Each poem must be of the poet’s own construction.


• Each poet gets three minutes (plus a ten-second grace period) to read one poem. If the poet exceeds the time limit, points will be deducted from the total score.


• The poet may not use props, costumes or musical instruments.


• Each poet sends in their entry before a deadline - 2 weeks in prior, which are adjudicated by the judges.

THE QUOTIDIAN

Event type: International Press (Journalism)


To emphasise on the power of writing and observation, we bring the Quotidian where the students can foster their ability to explain and refine their ideas


A participant shall have to cover The Joust and come up with articles to summarize the events.


● Each participant to submit a minimum of two articles related to the Joust


● Should be covering at least two events

RESEAU

Event type: International Press (Photography)


To emphasize on the power of photography and observation, we bring the Reseau where the students can foster their hobby of changing their vision into amazing ideas.


Judging criteria: Same as that of any International Press Photography event.
