JIIT
Jaypee Institute of Information Techology, Noida was established in the year 2001 and has been declared as a “Deemed to be University” under Section 3 of UGC Act 1956. The undergraduated programs of the University have been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation of AICTE. The Jaypee Institute of Information Technology has defined new innovative programs of learning to produce human resource capable of identifying analysing and solving problems.
The college is suitably located next to the Expo-Centre in Sector 62, Noida at the Delhi-Noida-Ghaziabad juncture. It has been developed as a modern world class campus, with intellectually vibrant ambience in a serene and lush green environment. JIIT gives an ample opportunity for reaching masses as it has many institutes and offices of International giants in the neighborhood.
THE JOUST
The Joust is Parola's Literary Fest that's been a continuing legacy, featuring various forms of literature and art. As the name speaks for itself, it's a battle of words where people come with their minds, wits and voices to speak for what they thoroughly believe in. The Joust gives a platform for students to discover how deeper they can go about an issue that has affected the society that they're a part of. It also helps them find their own potential and skills on how to go about talking about the issues, writing about them and coming up with the best possible solutions to those problems.
